Chocolate Chip Cookies



Classic chocolate chip cookies baked up browned and tender on sheets of parchment paper.

This recipe makes 4 dozen cookies.


Heres what you need:

  • Parchment Paper
  • 2½ cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon (Optional)
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 (12 ounce) bag semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup coarsely chopped nuts (optional)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Line cookie sheets with parchment paper; set aside
  3. On another sheet of parchment paper, combine flour, baking soda, salt and cinnamon (if using); set aside
  4. Beat together butter, brown sugar and granulated sugar in a large mixing bowl, with an electric mixer, until light and fluffy, then beat in eggs and vanilla
  5. Add flour mixture gradually to butter mixture; beat until well blended. Stir in chocolate chips and nuts (if using)
  6. Drop dough by rounded tablespoons onto parchment-lined cookie sheets
  7. Bake 10 to 12 minutes or until lightly browned
  8. Cool
