Pizza



Quick, Easy and Delicious-Homemade pizza crust and tomato sauce has never been easier-and this recipe for pepperoni pizza produces a delicious classic!

This recipe makes 2 12" pizzas, serves about 6 people, and is done in about 30 minutes!


Here's what you need

  • For the sauce
    • 6 ounces tomato paste
    • 1 teaspoon crushed dried oregano
    • 1 teaspoon crushed dried basil
    • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • For the crust
    • 3 1/4 cups all purpose flour, or more as needed
    • 0.5 ounces RapidRise yeast
    • 1 tablespoon sugar
    • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • 1 1/3 cups very warm water (120 - 130 degrees F)
    • 1/3 cups oil
  • Toppings
    • Use anything!
    • For the purpose of this recipe I will be using:
      • 1 (6 oz) package of pepperoni
      • ~1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

After you've gathered what you need, let's get started!

  1. For the sauce
    • Combine all sauce ingredients with 1/2 cup of water in a medium bowl and set aside to develop flavor while making crust
  2. For the crust
    • Combine 2 cups of flour with the yeast, sugar, and salt. Add the water and oil and mix until well blended (about 1 minute). Gradually add remaining flour until a soft, sticky ball is formed
    • Knead for about 4 minutes, on a floured surface, until dough is smooth and elastic. Add more flour, if needed
    • Let the dough rest for 10 minutes
    • Divide dough in half. Pat each half with floured hands into a 12" greased pizza pan OR roll dough to fit pans
  3. Preheat oven to 425F
  4. Top both crusts with sauce and toppings
  5. Then bake for 18 to 20 minutes, the crusts should be browned a cheese should be bubbly
  6. For best results, rotate the pans from top and bottom oven racks halfway through baking.
